Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

How long does a typical project take? +

It depends entirely on the scope of the project. A standard corporate website can take 2-4 weeks, while a complex web portal or mobile app might require 3-6 months. We set clear milestones and deadlines during the planning phase and stick to them strictly.

What's your pricing model? +

We believe in transparent pricing. Depending on your preference and project style, we offer fixed-price contracts for clearly defined projects, or a dedicated time-and-materials retainer for projects requiring ongoing evolution and agility.

Do you provide post-launch support? +

Absolutely. Launch day is just the beginning. We offer various tiered maintenance plans ensuring your platform is always updated, secure, and running at peak performance. We don't vanish once the final payment is made.

How do you ensure security? +

Security is integrated natively from day one. We adhere to OWASP standards, utilize high-grade encryption for all data transit and storage, ensure strict IAM roles on our server environments, and conduct regular penetration testing before going live.

Can you work with our existing systems? +

Yes. Our team has extensive experience building custom APIs and integrations to make your new platform play perfectly with your existing CRM, ERP, or legacy databases without disrupting your daily operations.
