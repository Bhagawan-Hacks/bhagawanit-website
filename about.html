Our Story

Started in a small office in Balaju, Kathmandu, Bhagawan IT was built on a simple premise: Nepali businesses deserve enterprise-grade digital products without the prohibitive price tags.

What began as a localized web design agency in Balaju has steadily grown into a **leading software development company in Nepal**, serving over 10+ businesses across the country. From ambitious startups in Kathmandu to established retail chains, we are recognized as the **Best Web Developer in Nepal**, bringing the same level of passion and technical rigor to every project.
