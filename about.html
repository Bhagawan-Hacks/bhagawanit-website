Our Story

Started in a small office in Kathmandu, Bhagawan IT was built on a simple premise: Nepali businesses deserve enterprise-grade digital products without the prohibitive price tags.

What began as a localized web design agency has steadily grown over 1 year to serve over 150+ businesses across Nepal. From ambitious startups to established retail chains and financial institutions, we bring the same level of passion, technical rigor, and security-first mindset to every project we undertake.
